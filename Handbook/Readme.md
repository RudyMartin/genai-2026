

## 📚 **AI Testing in Banking: A Hands-On Guide to Model Governance and SR 11-7 Compliance**

| Chapter | Topic                              | Banking Scenario                 | SR 11-7 Focus                              |
| ------- | ---------------------------------- | -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
